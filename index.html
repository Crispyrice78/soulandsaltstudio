Welcome to Soul & Salt Studio, where ocean-inspired resin art meets calm creativity. Each piece is a blend of fluid motion and mindful artistry — designed to bring the serenity of the sea into your home and spark a sense of peaceful creation within you.

About Angelika

I am a contemporary mixed media artist whose work captures the beauty and tranquility of the ocean. Since beginning my journey in 2020, I have developed a distinctive style that combines organically sourced materials such as sand, shells, and rocks to create immersive oceanic scenes that evoke a sense of peace and connection to nature.

Driven by a passion for both art and mental health advocacy, I explore the therapeutic power of creative expression and its ability to foster emotional healing. I am expanding my practice by offering art classes so you can create ocean-inspired work while experiencing the restorative benefits of making art.

Cutting Board Class

Bring the beauty of the sea to your kitchen! In this hands-on class, you'll design and create your own ocean-inspired resin cutting board. Using vibrant ocean hues and organic materials like shells and sand, you'll learn techniques to create a one-of-a-kind functional art piece that captures the movement and serenity of the shoreline. No experience is needed—just come ready to create, relax, and have fun! All materials are included, and you'll leave with a stunning cutting board that's perfect for display or entertaining.

Wall Art Class

Dive into creativity as you design your own ocean-inspired wall hanging! In this immersive art class, you'll create a stunning ocean scene using resin, sand, shells, and other natural elements. Learn how to layer color, texture, and movement to bring the beauty of the sea to life. No experience needed—just come ready to relax, explore and create. All materials are provided, and you'll leave with a gorgeous piece of coastal art to hang in your home or give as a meaningful gift.

Festive Ornaments

Join us for a fun and creative class where you'll learn step-by-step instructions to design two of your own beautiful, handcrafted ornaments! Whether you're adding a personal touch to your holiday décor or creating heartfelt gifts, this hands-on workshop—using glitters, resin, and jewels—lets your imagination shine. All materials are provided—just bring your creativity and holiday spirit! Perfect for all skill levels.
