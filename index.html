About Angelika

I am a contemporary mixed media artist whose work captures the beauty and tranquility of the ocean. Since beginning my journey in 2020, I have developed a distinctive style that combines organically sourced materials such as sand, shells, and rocks to create immersive oceanic scenes that evoke a sense of peace and connection to nature.

Driven by a passion for both art and mental health advocacy, I explore the therapeutic power of creative expression and its ability to foster emotional healing. I am expanding my practice by offering art classes so you can create ocean inspired work while experiencing the restorative benefits of making art.
